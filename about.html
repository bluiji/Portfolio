My Journey

From then on, I started watching tutorials and articles on how to code. However, due to lack of resources; insufficient hardware, and as kid whose curiosity is out of this world. Senior High School

After graduating High school, I pondered a lot on what strand I would pick for Senior High School. I was moving out to a different town and with the fact that I was unsure of my future career. One night, I while browsing my files, I saw my old files when I was first learning coding. Then, the passion I was once, the one I thought was just a phase came back. With then on, I was convinced what I would pursue a field where Programming will be my expertise. Even though it was still vague, it gave me a framework to work with. So during the summer break, I studied programming languages which further ignited my passion for coding. And from then on, I learned a lot of programming languages and logics that follows it.
